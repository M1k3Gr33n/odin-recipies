Tacosagna Recipe

A delicious lasagna containing all the wonderful ingrediants of a taco!

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1 small bell pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground red pepper
  • 1/2 tsp thyme, dried
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can beans, drained
  • 1 cup corn, canned or frozen
  • 1 pkg cream cheese
  • 1 pkgtortillas, flour
  • 1/4-1/2 cupcheese, shredded
