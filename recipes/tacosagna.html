Tacosagna Recipe

A delicious lasagna containing all the wonderful ingrediants of a taco!

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1 small bell pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground red pepper
  • 1/2 tsp thyme, dried
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can beans, drained
  • 1 cup corn, canned or frozen
  • 1 pkg cream cheese
  • 1 pkgtortillas, flour
  • 1/4-1/2 cupcheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Preheat ovent to 350 degrees.
  2. Brown meat with onions and peppers until no longer pink.
  3. Add all spices and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes, corn, and beans.
  5. Add the cream cheese ands stir until combined and melted.
  6. In an oven proof baking dish (I use a 9in. spring form pan) place a small spoonful of mixture on bottom (or olive oil) and then top with a tortilla. Now layer the mixture and tortillas.
  7. Bake in oven for about 20-25 minutes until melty and delicious.
